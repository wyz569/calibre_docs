Model Optimization With the VT5 Center GUI

VT5 Center finds the best fit for optical and resist models by running batches of experiments you design. You can automate the time-consuming process of developing optical models and VT5 resist models using the VT5 Center GUI (Litho > VT5 Center menu item) provided by Calibre WORKbench.

VT5 Center is a GUI that is designed to replace the legacy Model Flow GUI. It has been more closely modeled after CM1 Center, but maintains and improves on the controls found in the old Model Flow, Process Window, and Super Spreadsheet tools.

Each experiment optimizes a different model against common test pattern data. By providing VT5 Center with a criteria for comparing the optimized models, you instruct it to choose the best one available by those criteria.
