Jones Pupil View

For Jones pupil sources, eight graphs are displayed in the Jones Pupil View representing the real and imaginary components:
Figure 1. Viewing a Jones Pupil Source Map

  • Real — Re(Jxx), Re(Jxy), Re(Jyx), Re(Jyy) or Re(Jss), Re(Jsp), Re(Jps), Re(Jpp)

  • Imaginary — Im(Jxx), Im(Jxy), Im(Jyx), Im(Jyy) or Im(Jss), Im(Jsp), Im(Jps), Im(Jpp)

    Note:

    By default, the Jones Pupil View dialog box is loaded in Amplitude/Phase mode. Select the Re/Imag checkbox to switch to Real/Imaginary display mode.

    You can also use the XY checkbox at the bottom of the window to toggle between XY and SP coordinates display for Jones Pupil files.

Note:

The colormaps used to display a source map may appear to be off-center for sources that have centered features; however, this is an artifact of the drawing routine (the center pixel is drawn from 0,0 to +d,+d) and the coordinates are actually centered.
