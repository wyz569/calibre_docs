Optical Model Creation

Optical models are white box models; they rely on both accurate input and tuning to fit the empirical data. This chapter describes how to generate and tune an optical model.

An optical model is a mathematical representation of a lithographic exposure system; it models several constituents of the system such as illumination source, numerical aperture, lasing wavelength and lens aberration.
