Calibre Pattern Generator Modeling Test Patterns

The built-in modeling test patterns of the Calibre Pattern Generator are patterns and gauges suitable for modeling positive-tone develop (PTD) and negative-tone develop (NTD) processes.
Note:

The shift parameter is available for all pattern templates and shifts the patterns an equal distance both horizontally and vertically.

Adjustable_3_bar

Three vertical bars with one inner bar and two outer bars. One gauge measures the inner bar width.

Property Descriptions

L1, W1 — Length and width of inner bar

L2, W2 — Length and width of outer bars

S1 — Pitch between inner and outer bars

Gauge Descriptions

Gauge_0 — Width of inner bar
