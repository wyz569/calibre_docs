Calibre WORKbench Prerequisites

Calibre WORKbench and Calibre LITHOview have different prerequisites.

For Calibre LITHOview simulation functionality, you need:

  • A calibrated optical model

  • A calibrated process (resist and/or etch) model

  • A design file to run simulations on

For Calibre WORKbench model building functionality, you need:

  • A test pattern and accompanying spreadsheet containing measurement data

  • For optical models, settings for the wavelength, NA, and the characteristics of your film stack on the wafer

  • For resist or etch models, a sample spreadsheet file containing measurements of how a real world process affects the test pattern

  • Optionally, contour measurements taken using a SEM tool
