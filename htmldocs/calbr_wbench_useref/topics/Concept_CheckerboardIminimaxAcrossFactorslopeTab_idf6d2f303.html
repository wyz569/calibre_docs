Checkerboard (Imin/Imax Across Factor/Slope) Tab

The Imin/Imax across Factor/Slope tab (also known as the Checkerboard tab) shows a 4-dimensional plot of the empirical threshold (EmpTh) slope, factor, Imin, and Imax values.

The slope and factor are divided using a matrix of an equal range for slope (on the Y-axis) and factor (on the X-axis) as a 4x4 “checkerboard” style. You use this to see the points that have been simulated for each range category.

Populate the tab by loading data in the Bivariate Plot tab. The checkerboard plot runs a plot of all open files, adjusting the ranges to fit all the filesets.

Tip

Similar to the Bivariate Plots tab, the Checkerboard tab allows you to select one or more points and then click the Mark button. Points selected from the Checkerboard tab appear on layer 101 by default.
