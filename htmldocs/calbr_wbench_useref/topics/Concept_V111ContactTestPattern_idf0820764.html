V11.1 Contact Test Pattern

The V11.1 Contact test pattern provides flexibility and allows for accurate MEEF extraction.

It includes:

  • Pitch contact rows designed to fit a constant pitch concept. For pitch contact rows, each column reflects a constant pitch. In the past each column reflected a constant space.

  • Contact size increment of 5 nm around the target.

  • A maximum pitch that does not exceed the optical diameter.

  • Array size variation.

  • Added rectangular contacts.

To generate the V11.1 test pattern, you must supply the:

  • CD(w) — Target CD width. This value should be less than or equal to 150 nanometers.

  • P(min) — Minimum Pitch.

  • P(c) — Critical Pitch.

These values have the relationship Space: S(min) = P(min) – CD(w)
