Calibre SmartFill Flow

Adding fill to your layout using Calibre SmartFill begins with a DRC-clean layout and ends with a DRC-clean layout with fill.

Figure 1 shows the basic flow.

Figure 1. Basic Flow for Adding Fill

Table 1. Stages of Adding Fill

Stage

Description

1

Create or access a rule file for running Calibre SmartFill.

2

After your design is DRC clean, run Calibre SmartFill to add fill. Your run mode choices are:

  • Calibre DFM Command Line Invocation

  • Invocation of Calibre DFM from Calibre Interactive

3

Use Calibre RVE for DFM in conjunction with your layout viewer or editor to review any DRC errors. Depending on the number and type of errors you discover, you either repair the problems in your layout viewer, or modify your fill specification and generate a revised fill layer.

4

After DRC passes on all layers, save the layout and fill layer(s).

5

Backannotate the fill into your original design. See “Via and Fill Backannotation” in the Calibre Layout Comparison and Translation Guide.

If engineering change orders (ECOs) necessitate regenerating sections of your fill layers, consider using ecofill for this purpose.
