Calibre Critical Area Analysis Tutorial and Example Kit

Includes documentation and data for performing Critical Area Analysis (CAA) with Calibre YieldAnalyzer. The procedures step you through running CAA on a chip, CAA with marker layers, CAA on a cell library, and CAA with No Defect Density (NDD).

Go to this page on Support Center to download the eKit (Documentation tab, Document Types=Getting Started Guide). The link goes to the latest release.
