Spreadsheet Converters

You can convert CSV-formatted spreadsheets into SPICE or GDS and OASIS for use in Calibre 3DSTACK assembly and verification flows.

For information on creating and managing existing netlists, see the “System Netlist Generator”.

The following converters are available:

  • 3dstack::ss2gds
  • 3dstack::ss2oasis
  • 3dstack::ss2spice
  • 3dstack::xsi2gds
  • 3dstack::xsi2oasis
  • 3dstack::xsi2spice
