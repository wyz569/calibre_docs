Calibre SMO Overview

Source-Mask optimization accomplishes simultaneous source and mask optimization for each design. Optimization maximizes the imaging process window. To that end, the Calibre SMO solution leverages emerging flexibility of scanners to deliver pixelated illumination and adjusts optical properties for each individual illumination pixel.

The two parts of SMO work together to achieve this.

  • RET Selection integrates the point tools you would otherwise need to individually set up and run. It provides application-level integration, error checking, and preconfigured best practices. It also makes it easy to compare results across experiments.

  • Calibre pxSMO optimizes the source. It uses unconstrained solvers to produce the best possible combination of source and mask for a particular layout. The optimization is guided by a mix of objectives, grouped into jobs. It outputs thin mask or DDM optical models, depending on the style of input model.
