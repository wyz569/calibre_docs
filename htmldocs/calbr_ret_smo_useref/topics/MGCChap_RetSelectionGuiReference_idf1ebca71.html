RET Selection GUI Reference

To help you access functionality, this chapter lists the major windows, tabs, fields, and menus of the RET Selection GUI. All panes of the window are able to be undocked, rearranged, and stacked with other panes.

The figures use the default layout; your particular workspace may be arranged differently.
