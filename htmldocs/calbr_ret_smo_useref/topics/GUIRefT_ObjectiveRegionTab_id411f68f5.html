Objective Region Tab

To access: In the Session Browser, activate a session and select any Job under pxSMO or pxOPC. In the Job setup pane, click the Objective Region tab.

The Objective Region tab assigns process window conditions to objective regions.

Description

The Objective Region tab manages custom objectives assigned to marker layers at specified process window conditions. Adding a marker layer with objectives opens the Objective Region dialog box.

job names

Objectives and objective weights for optimizing the marker layer.

  • epe — Optimizes the mask to minimize the sum of edge placement error (EPE).

  • slope — Optimizes the mask to maximize the image slope.

  • eperms — Optimizes the mask to minimize the total area of edge placement error (EPE). Useful for layers with line end features.

  • extraprint — Optimizes the mask to minimize extra printing.

  • contour_diff — Optimizes the mask to minimize contour differences between the specified process window conditions. At least two process window conditions must be specified in the Objective Region table.

  • The individual job names apply the default objectives for the specified job. The listed job names differ for pxSMO and pxOPC.
