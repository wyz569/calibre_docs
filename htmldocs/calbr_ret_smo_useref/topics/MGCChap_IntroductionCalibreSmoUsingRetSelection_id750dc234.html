Introduction to Calibre SMO Using RET Selection

The Calibre® source-mask optimization (SMO) solution extends existing processes with next-generation computation lithography that enhances the imaging capability of lithographic systems used in the manufacturing of integrated circuits.

The Calibre SMO solution has two parts: the Calibre® pxSMO™ engine to perform the optimization, and the RET Selection interface to make it easy to configure, run, and choose the best source.
