Calibre nmMPC Operations

Calibre WORKbench supports a number of core Calibre nmMPC operations. For instance, there are different types of analysis that can be performed on the results of an MPC run, as well as options to adjust how data is displayed.

The operations and where their controls are located are summarized in the following table.
