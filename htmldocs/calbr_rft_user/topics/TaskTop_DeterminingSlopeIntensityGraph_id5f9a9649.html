Determining the Slope of an Intensity Graph

The slope of an intensity graph provides useful information about the sharpness of an image. This information is available in the Cutline tool.

Prerequisites

  • A cutline must be rendered in the Cutline tool (see “Drawing a Cutline”).

Procedure

  1. In the Cutline tool, click the Mark Slope button.
  2. Click a point on the cutline graph. This instructs Calibre to calculate and display the slope at the point selected.
