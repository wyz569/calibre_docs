SVRF Statements Not Mapped to TVF

The PEX Inductance family of statements is not mapped to TVF. This is the only family of specification statements for which this is true. Most layer operations are not mapped to TVF commands.

The following layer operations are not mapped to TVF even though the initial keyword is listed in Table 1 in “SVRF Statements Mapped to Compile-Time TVF”:

Expand Edge

Flatten

Snap

Expand Text

Push

 

Layer operations are handled with the tvf::SETLAYER or tvf::OUTLAYER commands.
