LVS Netlist Parsing Errors and Warnings

When a SPICE netlist is used as input, a transcript of any errors and warnings found during parsing is listed near the top of the LVS Report file.

For descriptive information about these messages, refer to the “LVS Results” chapter in the Calibre Verification User’s Manual. Errors cause LVS to abort, but warnings do not.
