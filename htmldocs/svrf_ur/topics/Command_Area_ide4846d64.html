Area

Layer operation

Selects polygons that meet an area constraint.

Usage

AREA layer constraint

Arguments

layer

A required original layer or layer set, or a derived polygon layer.

constraint

A required constraint listed in the “Constraint Notation” column of Table 1 in the “Constraints” topic. The constraint is interpreted in user units squared.

Description

Selects all layer polygons having areas that conform to the constraint.

Depending on your methodology, a narrow constraint range like > a < b, may be preferable to a constraint like == a.

See also Not Area.
