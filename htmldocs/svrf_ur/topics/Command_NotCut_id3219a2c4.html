Not Cut

Layer operation

Selects polygons that share all or none of their internal areas with polygons from a second layer. Optional constraints modify the selection criteria.

Usage

NOT CUT layer1 layer2 [constraint [BY NET] [EVEN | ODD]]

Arguments

layer1

A required argument specifying an original layer or layer set, or a derived polygon layer. The operation selects polygons from this layer that meet the conditions of the operation.

layer2

A required argument specifying an original layer or layer set, or a derived polygon layer.

constraint [BY NET] [EVEN | ODD]

An optional argument that places conditions on the number of layer2 polygons that cut a layer1 polygon. If the number of layer2 polygons or nets that cut a layer1 polygon does not satisfy the constraint, the layer1 polygon is selected. A layer1 polygon that is cut by zero layer2 polygons is always selected.

The constraint is one of those listed in the “Constraint Notation” column of Table 1 in the “Constraints” topic. Not Cut with a constraint of == 0 returns all layer1 polygons; use Cut instead.

  • BY NET — Specifies that the constraint applies to the number of distinct nets in the set of layer2 polygons that cut a layer1 polygon. Connectivity is required on layer2.

  • EVEN | ODD — Specifies that the number of layer2 polygons that cut the layer1 polygon is even or odd, respectively. The layer1 polygon is selected if the constraint is not met. For example, if a constraint of “> 1 EVEN” is specified, a layer1 polygon that is cut by three layer2 polygons is selected, while a layer1 polygon that is cut by four layer2 polygons is not selected.

Description

Selects layer1 polygons that share all or none of their internal areas with layer2 polygons. This is the complement of the Cut operation. If layer2 is empty, the Not Cut operation generates output equivalent to the polygon data on layer1.

Not Cut selects polygons that do not satisfy a Cut operation with the same arguments. If a constraint is specified, the constraint and optional keywords specify conditions for the complementary Cut operation. Polygons on layer1 that do not satisfy the conditions as applied to the complementary Cut operation are selected.

See also Not Interact.
