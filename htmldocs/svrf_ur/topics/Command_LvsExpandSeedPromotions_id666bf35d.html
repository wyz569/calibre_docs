LVS Expand Seed Promotions

Specification statement

Specifies whether to expand hcells that were subject to seed promotion during hierarchical device recognition. Used only in Calibre nmLVS-H and Calibre PERC.

Usage

LVS EXPAND SEED PROMOTIONS {NO | YES}

Arguments

NO

Keyword that specifies not to expand hcells that were subject to seed promotion during device recognition. This is the default behavior if you do not specify this statement.

YES

Keyword that specifies to expand hcells that were subject to seed promotion during hierarchical device recognition.

Description

Instructs the hierarchical netlist parser whether to expand hcells that were subject to seed promotion during device recognition. Seed promotion occurs when hierarchical device recognition is unable to completely form devices within a cell. Seed promotion moves those device shapes out of the cell and places them at a higher level of hierarchy where the device can be fully formed. When YES is specified, cells that were subject to seed promotion out of the cell do not serve as hcells, and so such cells are expanded. This statement may appear only once in a rule file.

Tip

Seed promotion can be mitigated by using layer operations that tend to preserve hierarchy when deriving device seed shapes. See “Hierarchy Preservation by Layer Operations” in the Calibre Verification User’s Manual for details.

The Calibre SPICE netlister places *.SEEDPROM statements in all subcircuits that were subject to seed promotion. This is done regardless of the LVS Expand Seed Promotions setting.

This statement operates on hcells specified explicitly (for example, in an hcell list) as well as implicitly (for example, with ‑automatch). LVS Box cells are not expanded by this statement.

Hcells expanded by this statement are indicated with warnings in the circuit comparison transcript. For example:

WARNING:  Cells "nand" and "nand" expanded due to seed promotion - correspondence ignored.

See also LVS Show Seed Promotions, LVS Use Careful AND, and AND LVSCAREFUL.

Examples

// Do not expand hcells in which seed promotions occur.
LVS EXPAND SEED PROMOTIONS NO
