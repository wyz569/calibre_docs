Keyword Abbreviations

You can abbreviate some of the words in an SVRF keyword set. Abbreviations are warranted when the word is long and cumbersome or its expected frequency of use is high. Abbreviations take the form of some prefix of the word to be abbreviated. They are also selected so as not to conflict with common layer names. Abbreviations are generally shown when the keyword is first introduced and are indicated by a subset of the keyword’s letters shown in uppercase.

For example, DEVice indicates the Device keyword can be abbreviated as Dev.
