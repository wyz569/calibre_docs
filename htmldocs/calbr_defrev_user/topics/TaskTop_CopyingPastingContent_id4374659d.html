Copying and Pasting Content

You can copy and paste content from the Defect List window to spreadsheet programs.

Procedure

  1. Select the contents from the Defect List by holding the left button of the mouse and dragging a box around the items to copy.
  2. Press Ctrl-c to copy.
  3. Paste the result into an open spreadsheet using Ctrl- v. The contents are copied into the spreadsheet.
