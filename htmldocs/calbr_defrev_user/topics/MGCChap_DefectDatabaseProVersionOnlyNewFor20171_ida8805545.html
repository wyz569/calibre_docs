Defect Database

Mask Inspection data comes from different sources: blank manufacturers, mask houses, wafer and production facilites. The data must pass through different stages of inspection and analysis. The Calibre DefectReview defect database enables you to manage, analyze, and generate all information required to track and manage defects. Once inspections and defect attributes are stored in the database, they can be used for further analysis in the future such as region analysis, defect source analysis, and blank defect transfer analysis.

For further information on defect trend analysis, refer to “Using Defect Management”.

Note:

You should have MySQL® Community Server v5.7.11 installed as a database server.
