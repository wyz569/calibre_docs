Repeatability Analysis

A mask is used multiple times for several IC (integrated circuit) fabrications with same circuit. During its lifetime, a mask is inspected for defects at regular interval. The Repeatability Analysis capability in Calibre DefectReview provides analysis results among successive inspection reports, such as information about added defects or changes in defect size.
