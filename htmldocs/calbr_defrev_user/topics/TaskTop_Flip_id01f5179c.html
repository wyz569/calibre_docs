Flip

You can flip (reflect) the defect inspection data around the X and Y axes in Calibre DefectReview.

The Flip transformation X-Dir and Y-Dir transform the inspection coordinate data across the corresponding axis. For example, the X-Dir selects the X-axis as the reflection axis and transforms the coordinates. Only the Y-coordinate is changed. Similarly, the Y-Dir selects the Y-axis as the reflection axis and transforms the coordinates; only the X-coordinate is changed.

Procedure

  1. In the Calibre DefectReview window, click Transform > Flip.
  2. In the Flip sub-menu, select the axis (X-Dir, Y-Dir) to flip the defect inspection data.
    Note:

    The system coordinates of a defect are updated in the Defect List as well.
