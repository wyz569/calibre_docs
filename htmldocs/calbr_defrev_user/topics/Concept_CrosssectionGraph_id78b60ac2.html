CrossSection Graph

Cross section analysis results can be configured to be displayed in any of the display units. To see the cross section results, select a display unit and then select the CrossSection option from the right click menu in the header of display unit .

Once the CrossSection option is selected, the cross section results are displayed and the Display Cross Section Bar and Cross Section Enabler buttons are enabled in the IMU window toolbar. Refer to “Cross Section Analysis” for more details about cross section analysis.
