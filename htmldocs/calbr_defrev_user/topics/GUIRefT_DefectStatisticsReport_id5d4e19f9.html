Defect Statistics Report

To access: On the Inspection Review Reports window, click Defect Statistics Report.

The Defect Statistics Report shows a distribution of the defects in terms of the number and percentage of defects by classification type.

Field

Description

Displays the number of defects detected.

Displays the defect count in terms of percentage of total defects.
