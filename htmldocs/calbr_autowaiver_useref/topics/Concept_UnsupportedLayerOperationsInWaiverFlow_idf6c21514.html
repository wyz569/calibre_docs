Unsupported Layer Operations in waiver_flow

Some layer operations are not supported in waiver_flow only. Results from such operations may have waiver shapes produced for them by other tools such as Calibre RVE.

In the DFM family of operations, DFM Analyze, DFM Copy, DFM Property, DFM RDB, or DFM Space are supported, but all others are not. The layer operations that are not supported by waiver_flow can have waiver shapes generated for them using Calibre RVE or drc_waiv_asc2gds.
