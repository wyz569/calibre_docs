Waiver File Descriptions

The waiver setup, waiver criteria, and waiver cells files control how waivers are generated and applied in batch mode. These files are also used to control Calibre Interactive and options in the Calibre RVE Export Waived Results window. The output files produced by Calibre Auto-Waivers depend on whether you are generating waivers or applying waivers and on the type of verification run.
