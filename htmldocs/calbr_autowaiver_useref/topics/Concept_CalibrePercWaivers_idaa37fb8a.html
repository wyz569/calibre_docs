Calibre PERC Waivers

The Calibre Auto-Waivers license also enables waivers for Calibre® PERC™.

Calibre PERC supports waivers in two contexts: topology waivers, which are based upon netlists, and geometry waivers for Logic-Driven Layout (LDL results). See “Calibre PERC Waiver Flows” in the Calibre PERC User’s Manual for details about both waiver types.
