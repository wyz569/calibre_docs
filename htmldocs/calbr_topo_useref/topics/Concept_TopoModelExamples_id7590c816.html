Topo Model Examples

Topography models contain mostly parameters based on the signal information you are interested in modeling.

Topo Model Concepts

  • All linear and quadratic parameters specified in the initial topo model are optimized.

  • A topo model can be created using a modelform or manually. They are commonly referenced by their modelform codes.
