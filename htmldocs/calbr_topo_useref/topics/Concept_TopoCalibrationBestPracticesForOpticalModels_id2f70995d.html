Topo Calibration Best Practices for Optical Models

Optical models are calibrated as the first step of the topo calibration flow. They are calibrated using only planar gauges, in order to best characterize the optical source.

Use these best practice recommendations to calibrate optical models.

Test Pattern Guidelines for Optical Models

Use 200 to 400 planar gauge features. Planar gauges are the gauges that do not contain transitional effects. These are gauges that are typically deep inside the oxide and silicon regions.
