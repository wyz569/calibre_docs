Topographical Modeling User’s Guide

Topographical modeling models the reflection effects from underlying resist topography caused by the absence of anti-reflection coatings in implant layers. The result of this methodology is a set of optical models that predict the characteristics of different films under the resist with a set of signals that model secondary reflection and diffraction from these films.
