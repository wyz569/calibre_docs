Fragments and Edges

A fragment is an edge or part of an edge.

The term edge refers to a line segment between two corners. Figure 1 presents a simplified representation of two edges on a polygon.

Figure 1. Edge Illustration

A fragment can be an entire edge or part of an edge in a layout polygon (see Figure 2). Fragments are also called edge segments, especially within the SVRF documentation.
