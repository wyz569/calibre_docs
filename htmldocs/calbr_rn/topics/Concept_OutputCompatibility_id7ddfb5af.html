Output Compatibility

It is our objective to keep the release output consistent from release to release. However, some releases will have differences due to changes in the default settings and algorithms that improve the functionality of the tools. These differences are documented here. It is the responsibility of each customer to use this information as applicable to his or her design methodology.
Note:

OPC Results Differ Between AOI and AOJ Calibre Trees

Differences may be observed in the results from OPC jobs that are run using an AOI and an AOJ Calibre tree. The differences in results are due to the different hardware processors and operating system libraries that are supported by the different Calibre trees.
