Tracking Support Cases, DRs, and ERs

If you are a registered Support Center user, you can open a new support case and view the status of your own support cases, including the status of any attached Defect Report (DR) or Enhancement Request (ER). You can also view all support cases for your site, including all associated DRs and ERs.

https://support.sw.siemens.com/support-case

To be kept informed of the progress of a particular DR or ER, open a support case and indicate the DR or ER number you are interested in.
