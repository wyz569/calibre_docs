Viewing Calibre PERC LDL Results in Calibre RVE

The Calibre PERC Logic Driven Layout Current Density (LDL CD) and Point-to-Point (LDL P2P) applications produce a DFM Database and one or more DRC results databases in ASCII format. Calibre RVE for PERC allows you to view, sort, and filter results, highlight results to an attached layout viewer, and view a rendered schematic in the Internal Schematic Viewer.
