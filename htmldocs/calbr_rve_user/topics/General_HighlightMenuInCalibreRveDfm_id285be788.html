Highlight Menu in Calibre RVE for DFM

The Highlight menu in Calibre RVE for DFM has one DFM-specific selection, Highlight Net/Device/Pin.

Highlight > Highlight Net/Device/Pin — Opens the Highlight Layout Net/Device/Pin Dialog Box.

The other general Highlight menu selections are described in the section “Highlight Menu in Calibre RVE”.
