Net Highlight Right-Click Menu

Net-specific highlighting options are available from the context menu when you right-click a net.

Access the menu with one of the following methods in the Detailed View in Calibre RVE for LVS or the Result Data Pane in Calibre RVE for PERC:

  • From Extraction Results, Comparison Results, or PERC Results tab — Right-click a net and choose Highlight.

  • From the P2P or CD tab for Calibre PERC LDL results — Right-click a net and choose Layout cell cell_name> Highlight.

The Highlight sub-menu selections are described in the following table. However, the Highlight in Cell menu item is first in the top-level right-click menu for certain LVS discrepancy types, rather than a sub-menu item.

Table 1. Net Highlight Right-Click Menu in Calibre RVE

Menu Item

Information

Devices on Net

Highlight devices on the net. The filters on the Highlights tab are used.

Device-pins on Net

Highlight device pins on the net. The filters on the Highlights tab are used.

 

In order for Calibre RVE to show device pins, you must output pin locations in the SVDB. To output pin locations, include the CCI or PINLOC keywords in the Mask SVDB Directory statement.

 

Calibre RVE does not issue a warning message when the SVDB does not include pin location information.

Ports on Net

Highlight ports on the net. The filters on the Highlights tab are used.

 

Calibre defines ports as objects created by the Port Layer Text and Port Layer Polygon statements. Also see “Highlighting Ports in the Layout”, and the “Usage Notes” section in “Info Pane” regarding the “Ports on net” entry.

Highlight in Cell

Highlight the net in the context cell only, not including lower level cells and instances.

In some cases the Highlight in Lower Cell Dialog Box is displayed.

Highlight in Area

Select one of the following commands:

  • Highlight Net – Restrict highlighting to an area selected in the layout viewer.

  • Highlight by Layer – Restrict highlighting to an area selected in the layout viewer and split the highlight into its constituent layers.

  • Highlight on Layer – Restrict highlighting to an area selected in the layout viewer and open a dialog box to select the layers to highlight the net on.

Highlight by Layer

Highlight the net according to the different layers. The filters on the Highlights tab are used.

Highlight on Layer

Open a dialog box to select the layers to highlight the net on. The layer settings are initialized from the layer filter options on the Highlights tab. Layer settings for each net are saved during the session, but are not saved on exit.
