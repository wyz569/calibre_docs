Highlighting Devices on a Net

There are two methods for highlighting the devices that are connected to a layout or source net.

Use one of the following methods:

  • For a layout net, right-click the layout net in the detailed view and choose Highlight > Devices on Net.

  • For a source net, right-click the source net in the detailed view and choose Highlight Devices on Net. The source netlist must be available.

See the section “Isolating Shorts Between Untexted Nets” for an application of this feature.
