Parasitics Toolbar

The parasitics toolbar is only available when the Parasitics tab is active.
Figure 1. Parasitics Toolbar

Table 1. Parasitics Toolbar Contents

Icon

Action

Description

Show Net Info

Opens a tree view of the net in the Info Pane.

Highlight Net

Highlights the net in the attached layout and schematic viewers.

Show Parasitics on Net

Opens a detailed view of the parasitics on the net.
