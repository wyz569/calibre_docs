Configuring Calibre RDB Conversion to Native Place and Route Format

You can configure conversion options relating to cell, rule check, and side RDB inclusion when converting results in a Calibre RDB to native Place and Route formats. You can also specify options to control the offset, magnification, and minimum feature size of the converted shapes.
