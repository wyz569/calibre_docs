Using Calibre RVE for DFM

The Calibre RVE for DFM interface allows you to browse the contents of a DFM database. The DFM report card is integrated with Calibre RVE for DFM and enables you to browse errors by chip, by cell, and by window and to generate visual error representations in the form of color maps and histograms.
