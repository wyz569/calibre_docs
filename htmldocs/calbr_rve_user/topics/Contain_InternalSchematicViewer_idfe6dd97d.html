Internal Schematic Viewer

The Internal Schematic viewer provides schematic viewing for source and layout using schematic rendering technology. You enable and disable the Internal Schematic Viewer with View > Schematics and the “Show netlist schematics on highlighting” and “Show Schematic Pane” settings on the Options tab in the Schematic Viewer category.
