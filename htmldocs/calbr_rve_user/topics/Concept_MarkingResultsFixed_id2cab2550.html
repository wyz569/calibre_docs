Marking Results as Fixed

To mark a result or cluster as fixed, right-click the selection, and choose Fix > Fix. You can also use the F keyboard keys to mark results as fixed.

Waived results are indicated with a green check mark over a dim red X ().

You can click the filter icon ()and select a filter to display only fixed or unresolved results.
