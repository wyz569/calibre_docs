Saving Calibre RVE Settings and Options

Changes you make to Calibre RVE settings are automatically saved when you exit Calibre RVE. The settings are restored the next time you start Calibre RVE. Database-specific options are saved and restored in Calibre RVE for DRC and Calibre RVE for PERC.
