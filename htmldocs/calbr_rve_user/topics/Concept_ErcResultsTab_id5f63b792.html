ERC Results Tab

The ERC Results tab is available if you ran ERC checks in your Calibre nmLVS run. Open the ERC Results tab by clicking “ERC Results” in the Navigator, or choosing View > Results > ERC > ERC Database.

The ERC Results tab uses the DRC interface, as shown in Figure 1.

Figure 1. ERC Results Tab

The left side of the ERC Results tab contains a tree view of the checks that were run, and the right side displays the individual check results. See the chapter “Using Calibre RVE for DRC” for detailed information on using the DRC Results tab.

If additional output files or RDBs were created as part of the run, they will be also be listed in the ERC section of the Navigator.

You can waive ERC results with the same method used for DRC results; see “Marking Results as Fixed”.

See “Specifying Automatic Waivers for ERC” in the Calibre Interactive User’s Manual for information on running Calibre nmLVS with ERC waivers.
