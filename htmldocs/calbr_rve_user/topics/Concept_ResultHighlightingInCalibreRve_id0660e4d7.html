Result Highlighting in Calibre RVE

Highlighting results in a connected layout or schematic viewer is an important part of the physical verification process. Calibre RVE provides many options to simplify the viewing of results from a Calibre run.

Highlight results — Results are typically highlighted by one of these methods:

  • Double-click a result (DRC).

  • Click in a colored design element (LVS and PERC), or netlist icon (PEX).

  • Right-click a result for a context-sensitive highlight menu (all applications).

Erase highlights — Click the clear highlights button on the standard toolbar or choose Highlight > Clear Highlights from the menu bar to erase all highlights in the connected viewers.

Set highlight options — See Calibre RVE Highlight Options.

The Connection icon in the lower right corner of the session window (see Figure 1) indicates the status of the socket connection between Calibre RVE and the viewers selected on the Setup > Design Tools option pane. Click the Connection icon to open a drop-down menu with selections to connect, disconnect, or open the options pane. The Connection icon is initially gray; it becomes colored when a connection is made to an external tool, or red if the connection attempt fails.
