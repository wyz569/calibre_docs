Waived Results from Cloned Cells

Results from cloned cells are waived in the original cell, Any result from a cloned cell which matches a waived result in the original cell is also waived when you reload the results database.

Suppose for example, that two cloned cells are produced during a run, each having an identical result in the original cell, and suppose you waive one of the results. When you close Calibre RVE and re-open the results database, both results are shown as waived. This is shown in Figure 1.

Figure 1. Waived Results from Cloned Cells

In order to unwaive a result, you must waive the matching result from all cloned cells, otherwise the results are still marked as waived when you reload the database. This occurs because whenever one result from a cloned cell is marked as waived, all matching results in other cloned cells are also waived. To aid in identifying matching results, enable “Classify result shapes” in the DRC Browser category on the Options tab and sort by the #SC property.
