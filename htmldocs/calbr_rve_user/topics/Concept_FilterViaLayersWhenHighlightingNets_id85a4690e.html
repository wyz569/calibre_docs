Filter Via Layers When Highlighting Nets

By default, contact layers appearing in a Connect … BY statement are not included in highlighting actions in Calibre RVE for LVS, PERC, PEX, and DFM. There are two methods for including the contact layers.

  • “Skip contact/via layers while highlighting” located on the Highlights Tab (Highlight > Setup Highlights) in Calibre RVE for LVS, PERC, and PEX.

  • “Skip contact/via layers while highlighting” located on the Options tab in the Highlighting category in the “Layout Highlighting” expandable section. (Setup Highlighting Options Pane) This option applies to Calibre RVE for LVS, PERC, PEX, and DFM.
