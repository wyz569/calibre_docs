Toolbars in Calibre RVE for PERC

The toolbars in Calibre RVE for PERC provide shortcuts to commonly used menu selections.

The Standard Toolbar in Calibre RVE is used for all Calibre RVE applications, while other toolbars are only available when the associated report tab is selected.
