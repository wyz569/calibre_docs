Net Highlighting

The right-click menu for nets includes selections to highlight devices, pins, and to highlight by layer. You can also emphasize a highlighted net.

See the section “Net Highlight Right-Click Menu” for an explanation of the selections in the net context menu.

To emphasize a net after highlighting it in the Internal Schematic Viewer, click the Mute Colors () button to easily find a highlighted element within a dense display. The Mute Colors button is in the top left corner of the schematic display and causes all schematic elements except the highlighted element to be displayed in gray.

To emphasize a net that has been highlighted in the Calibre DESIGNrev layout viewer, right-click the highlight layer in the Layers palette and enable “Emphasize Selected.”

Tip for Calibre DESIGNrev Users

If you use Calibre DESIGNrev as your layout viewer, you can use it to save the polygons that make up a highlighted net as an RDB, as described in the video “How To Save Net Polygons as an RDB Using Calibre RVE and Calibre DESIGNrev.”



Or, see the topic “Saving Highlighted Shapes as a Calibre nmDRC Results Database (RDB)” in the Calibre DESIGNrev Layout Viewer’s Manual.
