Calibre DESIGNrev Navigation Features

Navigating a large design layout can present challenges due to a limited viewing space. Calibre DESIGNrev provides several different methods, such as zooming and panning capabilities, Cells Browser, and Bookmarks, for adjusting your view to the appropriate locations in your layout.
