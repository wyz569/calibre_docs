Pushing Toplevel Results to Lower Level Cells

In some cases, DRC violations occur at the toplevel of a design due to interblock interactions that must be fixed at the block level.

Prerequisites

  • A layout with some toplevel DRC violations. For the purposes of this procedure, assume you have a layout named simple.oas.

  • A DRC results database named flat.rdb with layers that represent the extent of each cell.

Procedure

  1. Open simple.oas in Calibre DESIGNrev and review the hierarchy.
