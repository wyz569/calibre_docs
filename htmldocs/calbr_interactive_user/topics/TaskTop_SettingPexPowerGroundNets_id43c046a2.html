Setting PEX Power and Ground Nets

You can configure the PEX Power and PEX Ground statements for specific nets using Calibre Interactive PEX.

Corresponding commands: PEX Power and PEX Ground. These settings are only used for Calibre xL.

Procedure

  1. Click the PEX Options button on the left panel, or choose Setup > PEX Options if the button is not present.
  2. Click the Misc tab.
  3. In the fields for “Power nets” and “Ground nets,” select “LAYOUT” or “SOURCE” as the source for the net names, then enter one or more net names; separate multiple net names with a space.
