Check Selection in Calibre Interactive nmDRC and Calibre Interactive DFM

The Check Selection Recipe specifies the checks that are executed during a Calibre Interactive nmDRC or Calibre Interactive DFM run. The Check Selection Recipe is specified on the Rules pane.
