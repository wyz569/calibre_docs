Ignoring Ports

Choose this option to disable the use of cell ports as initial correspondence points for connectivity comparison.

Procedure

  1. Click the LVS Options button on the left panel to display the Options pane. If the LVS Options button is not available, choose Setup > LVS Options.
  2. Click the Supply tab.
  3. Enable “Ignore layout and source ports during comparison.”
