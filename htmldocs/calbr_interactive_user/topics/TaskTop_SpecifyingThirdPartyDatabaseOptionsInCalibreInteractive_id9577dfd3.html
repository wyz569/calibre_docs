Specifying Third Party Database Options in Calibre Interactive

You can set several Calibre Interactive options that affect how your OpenAccess or LEF/DEF design is read.

Procedure

  1. Click app Options on the left panel, where app is one of DRC, LVS, DFM, PEX, or PERC. If the app Options button is not present, choose Setup > app Options from the menu.
  2. Click the Database tab.
  3. Click the OpenAccess/LEFDEF sub-tab.

    For information on the tab contents, refer to “Third Party Database Options”.
