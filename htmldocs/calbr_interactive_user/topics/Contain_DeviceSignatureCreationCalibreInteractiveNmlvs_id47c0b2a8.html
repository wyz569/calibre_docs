Device Signature Creation with Calibre Interactive nmLVS

Calibre Interactive nmLVS allows you to easily generate unique signature strings for devices. Device signatures enable the matching of devices in the layout to a specific geometric pattern, rather than matching to just the layers present.

For information on device signatures, signature generation, and signature comparisons, refer to “Device Signatures” in the Calibre Verification User’s Manual and the Device command in the SVRF Manual. It will help if you are familiar with device signature concepts as you fill out the Edit Parameters for Device Dialog Box.
