Calibre Interactive with Calibre DESIGNrev or WORKbench

To start Calibre Interactive from Calibre DESIGNrev or Calibre WORKbench, choose Verification and choose the desired interface.

Refer to “Invoking Calibre Interactive From a Layout or Schematic Viewer” for more information on how to invoke Calibre Interactive from Calibre DESIGNrev.

You can also define a trigger for Calibre Interactive in Calibre DESIGNrev; see “External Trigger Definitions in Calibre DESIGNrev”.
