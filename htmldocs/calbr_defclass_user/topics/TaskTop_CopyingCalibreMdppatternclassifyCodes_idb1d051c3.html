Copying Calibre DefectClassify Codes

After Calibre DefectClassify completes its run, you can copy the classification codes to their respective manual classification columns in the Defect List of Calibre DefectReview.

Procedure

In the Miscellaneous pane of the Calibre DefectClassify window, click Copy MDPPattern Classifications.
Figure 1. Copy MDPPattern Classifications

This option copies the generated classification codes to Calibre DefectReview into mapped columns in the Defect List. Refer to the Calibre DefectReview User’s Manual for further information.
