Finding the Length of a Net Path Connected to Gate

The two rule checks in this section measure the path lengths of nets connected to MOS gates on the first two layers of metal interconnect.

 

Topic

Summary

Finding the Length of a Net Path Connected to Gate: Effective Length Method

An effective length of each polygon in the net path is estimated using an equation.

Finding the Length of a Net Path Connected to Gate: Centerline Method

The polygons that are part of the net are broken into line and corner segments, and the centerlines are found. The centerline length is summed to find the path length.

Comparison of Centerline and Effective Length Methods for Finding Path Length

The results of the two length estimates are compared for a selection of shapes.
