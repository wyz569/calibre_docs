Current Density and Resistance Checks

In certain flows, it is desirable to calculate current density and interconnect resistivity for ESD and other types of checking. Current density and resistance is calculated between specified pins, or between ports and pins.
Figure 1. Current Density and Resistance

Details for writing these types of checks are found in the “Current Density Checks” and “Point-to-Point Resistance Checks” chapters of the Calibre PERC User’s Manual.
