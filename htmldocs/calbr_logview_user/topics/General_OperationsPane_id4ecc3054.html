Operations Pane

To access: Operations tab

The Operations pane displays job results by layer or operation. The results displayed in this pane are extracted from the “OPS COMPLETE” lines in the “EXECUTIVE MODULE” of the log file. You can sort any column on this pane in ascending or descending order by clicking on the column header.

Table 1 provides information on each of the fields available on this pane.
