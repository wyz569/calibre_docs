Constraint Keywords

Calibre nmSRAF requires the specification of some minimum SRAF dimensions for application of MRC rules.

For organizational keyword information, refer to “sraf_options”.

Table 1. Constraint Keywords

Keyword

Description
alignoriginInterprets canonical cell and tile origins in alignment with global database origins. Used by RET SBAR and RET MBSRAF.
extensionSpecifies SRAF extension length. Used by RET MBSRAF only.
maxlengthSpecifies the maximum length of generated SRAFs, and the combined maximum length of generated SRAFs of same priorities during cleanup. Used by RET SBAR and RET MBSRAF.
maxoffsetSpecifies the maximum distance from an SRAF to a target shape. Used by RET MBSRAF only.
maxoffsetnegativeSpecifies the maximum distance from a negative SRAF to the enclosing target shape edge. Used by RET MBSRAF only.
minareaSpecifies the minimum area for SRAFs. Used by RET SBAR and RET MBSRAF.
minareaextensionSpecifies the minimum area for 45-degree SRAF extensions. Used by RET SBAR and RET MBSRAF.
minfeaturespaceSpecifies the minimum distance from target shapes to an SRAF. Used by RET SBAR and RET MBSRAF.
minlengthSpecifies the minimum length allowed for a rectangular SRAF. Used by RET SBAR and RET MBSRAF.
minlengthextensionSpecifies the minimum length for 45-degree SRAF extensions. Used by RET SBAR and RET MBSRAF.
minoffsetSpecifies the minimum distances between generated SRAFs. Used by RET SBAR and RET MBSRAF.
minoffsetlayerspaceSpecifies the minimum distance to SRAF shapes from the offsetlayer. Used by RET SBAR and RET MBSRAF.
minsquareareaSpecifies the minimum area of a square SRAF. Used by RET SBAR and RET MBSRAF.
minsquarelengthSpecifies the minimum edge length for square SRAFs. Used by RET SBAR and RET MBSRAF.
minwidthSpecifies the minimum width allowed for square SRAFs. Used by RET SBAR and RET MBSRAF.
shieldingUses edge tag shielding to characterize SRAF generation. Used by RET MBSRAF only.
squarewidthSpecifies the width of square SRAFs. Used by RET MBSRAF only.
widthSpecifies the default width of generated SRAFs. Used by RET MBSRAF only.
