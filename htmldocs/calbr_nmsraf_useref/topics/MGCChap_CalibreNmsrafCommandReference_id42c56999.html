Calibre nmSRAF Command Reference

You define Calibre nmSRAF dimensional- or model-based templates to insert sub-resolution assist features (SRAFs) into the layout to achieve the best process window.

The placement of SRAFs depends on how you specify their constraints, models, existing layout topology, and cleanup methods.
