Cleanup Keywords

Calibre nmSRAF invokes SRAF cleanup after generation.

For organizational keyword information, refer to “sraf_options”.

Table 1. Cleanup Keywords

Keyword

Description
centermergeMerges two parallel SRAFs within the specified distance. Used by RET SBAR only.
cleanmodeSpecifies the method of SRAF removal. Used by RET SBAR only.
cleanupversionSpecifies the version of SRAF cleanup. Used by RET SBAR only.
cnsrafpromotionDisables default promotion for cnsraf. Used by RET SBAR only.
dynamicsizingSizes SRAFs during generation. Used by RET SBAR and RET MBSRAF.
dynamicsizing_ambiguousManages the sizing of SRAFs near tile boundaries during generation. Used by RET SBAR and RET MBSRAF.
dynamicsizingrunlengthDisables centermerge emphasis on long SRAFs. Used by RET SBAR only.
interpriorityalignAligns SRAFs of different priorities during cleanup. Used by RET SBAR and RET MBSRAF.
intrapriorityalignAligns SRAFs of same priorities during cleanup. Used by RET SBAR and RET MBSRAF.
lineendmergeMerges SRAF line-ends during SRAF cleanup. Used by RET SBAR and RET MBSRAF.
maxmoveRestricts SRAF movement during cleanup. Used by RET MBSRAF only.
reducedbarsplitSplits or merges SRAFs in conflict. Used by RET SBAR only.
