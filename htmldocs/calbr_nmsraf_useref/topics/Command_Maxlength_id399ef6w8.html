maxlength

Constraint Keywords

Specifies the maximum length of generated SRAFs, and the combined maximum length of generated SRAFs of same priorities during cleanup. Used by RET SBAR and RET MBSRAF.

Usage

maxlength value

Arguments

value

A required positive floating point number. The value is the maximum length of generated SRAFs, or the combined length of generated SRAFs of the same priority processed by intrapriorityalign during cleanup.

Description

An optional keyword that measures and splits generated SRAFs to a maximum of the specified length. The maxlength keyword may only be specified globally. You may use maxlength in conjunction with intrapriorityalign to limit the combined length of aligned and merged SRAFs. The maxlength keyword may be applied to positive and negative SRAF generation.

You may use maxlength for the following templates:

  • localmaxgradient

  • rect

  • rectcorner

  • rectdiag

  • rectedge

  • recthv
