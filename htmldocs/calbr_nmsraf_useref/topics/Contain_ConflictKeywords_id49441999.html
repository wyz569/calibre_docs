Conflict Keywords

Provides commands for managing conflicts formed during SRAF generation.

For organizational keyword information, refer to “sraf_options”.

Table 1. Conflict Keywords

Keyword

Description
centerthreshSpecifies the threshold of a conflict region. Used by RET SBAR only.
conflictmodeSpecifies the means of conflict region processing. Used by RET SBAR only.
maxconflictSpecifies the maximum size of a conflict region. Used by RET SBAR only.
oversizesrafAllows SRAFs within conflict regions to exceed the conflict region size. Used by RET SBAR only.
sbwidthSpecifies the width of secondary SRAFs. Used by RET SBAR only.
srafEnables and specifies the size of SRAF output in conflict regions. Used by RET SBAR only.
