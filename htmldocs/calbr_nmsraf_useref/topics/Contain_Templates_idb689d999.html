Templates

Calibre nmSRAF requires dimensional- or model-based templates to pattern and prioritize SRAF generation.

For organizational template information, refer to “sraf_options”.

Table 1. Templates

Keyword

Description
localmaxgradientCreates SRAFs around the local maxima of the gradient. Used by RET MBSRAF only.
pairtemplatePlaces SRAFs at specified angles and offsets from target shape center. Used by RET SBAR only.
rectPlaces a rectangular SRAF relative to the center of the target shape. Used by RET SBAR only.
rect45Places four rectangular SRAFs rotated 45 degrees relative to the target shape center. Used by RET SBAR only.
rect45cornerPlaces four rectangular SRAFs rotated 45 degrees at target shape corners. Used by RET SBAR only.
rectcornerPlaces SRAFs with four methods relative to corners of target shapes. Used by RET SBAR only.
rectdiagPlaces four or eight rectangular SRAFs diagonally offset relative to the center of the target shape. Used by RET SBAR only.
rectedgePlaces rectangular SRAFs offset from each edge of the target shape. Used by RET SBAR only.
recthvPlaces four rectangular SRAFs at orthogonal X,Y pitches relative to the target shape center. Used by RET SBAR only.
srafringPlaces square SRAFs radially around a target shape. Used by RET SBAR only.
srafringhvPlaces rectangular and square SRAFs radially around a target shape. Used by RET SBAR only.
