Calibre nmDRCgold

Licensing for Calibre® nmDRCgold.

Required License

  • calnmdrcgold (see Considerations and Exceptions)

Substitute License

  • See Considerations and Exceptions.

Also Enables

  • None.

Considerations and Exceptions

  • You can run any combination of the following with the calnmdrcgold license:

    • Calibre nmDRC or Calibre nmDRC-H

    • Calibre Pattern Matching

    • Calibre Multi-Patterning

    • Calibre Auto-Waivers Flow

  • calnmdrcgold is the preferred license in runs including Calibre nmDRC or Calibre nmDRC‑H and two or more of the products listed above. In other runs, individual product licenses are preferred, and you can use the calnmdrcgold license as a substitute.
