Calibre nmModelflow Overview

Process models are used to simulate the results of lithography designs on a wafer. As design sizes shrink, more complex models are needed to accurately predict and simulate the effects of those elements.

Calibre nmModelflow improves on the Calibre WORKbench modelflow_v2 and CM1 Center functionality by:

  • Basing the modeling flow around persistent database objects

  • Creating separate flow stages to calibrate models in phases

  • Supporting metrics capabilities, filters, plots, and dynamic process windows
