Editing Multiple Calibration Jobs

For situations where multiple calibration jobs need their components changed, use the Multiple Edit utility in Calibre nmModelflow to quickly adjust selected jobs.

Note:

This wizard is also used for the Copy Descendants button; all descendants of the selected job(s) are added to the list of edited jobs.

Prerequisites

  • Calibre nmModelflow invoked

  • Multiple calibration jobs defined in the Calibre nmModelflow database

Procedure

  1. Switch to the Calibration Job Manager tab.
  2. Select at least two calibration jobs and click Multiple Edit.


  3. In the Job Copy page, click any cell with a dropdown arrow to display the available choices in the database for that column.


    Make a selection for the cell, or click Apply to All to the right of the choice to apply the change to every item in the list.

    When you have finished making changes, click Next.

  4. Each job is listed as a separate Layer Mapping For CJ-x page. For each job selected, adjust the layer mapping as needed, then click Next.
  5. On the Finish page, inspect the commands generated to make the changes, and click Finish to run the changes.
