Biasing Methods

BIASRULE uses two methods to organize biasing commands and values.
Table 1. BIASRULE Biasing Methods

Keyword

Description
-ruleSpecifies the biasing of edges with a rule-based method per provided space and other ranges.
-tableSpecifies the biasing of edges with a table-based method per provided space and width ranges.

For organizational keyword information, refer to “BIASRULE”.
