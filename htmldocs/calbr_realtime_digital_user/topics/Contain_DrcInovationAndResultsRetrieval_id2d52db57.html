DRC Invocation and Results Retrieval

Commands in this group can be used to run Calibre nmDRC, retrieve DRC result statistics and result coordinate data, save results to RDB files, and retrieve results from RDB files.

Commands in this group do not update the Calibre RealTime Digital toolbar, with the exception of RunDRC and LoadResultsFromRDB. RunDRC may also highlight DRC results.

  • GetCheckInfo
  • GetResultCount
  • GetResultIdentifiers
  • GetResultCoordinates
  • GetResultInfo
  • GetResultSet
  • LoadResultsFromRDB
  • RunDRC
  • SaveResultsToRDB
