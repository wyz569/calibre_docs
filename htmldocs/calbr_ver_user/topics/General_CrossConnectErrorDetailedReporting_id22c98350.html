Cross-Connect Error Detailed Reporting

Cross-connects are detected when two layout nets are interchanged to two instances.
Figure 1. Cross-Connected Instances Error

When LVS Report Option FX is used, the error is reported as follows:

------------------------------------------------------------------------- 
                         Cross-connect error 

       X10 inv                                                  X30  inv 
         VSS: vss                                                  VSS: vss 
         VCC: vcc                                                  VCC: vcc 
         IN: 1                                                     IN: 1 
         OUT: out1                                                 ** out1 ** 
         ** out3 **                                                OUT: out3 

       X30  inv                                                  X10 inv 
         VSS: vss                                                  VSS: vss 
         VCC: vcc                                                  VCC: vcc 
         IN: 2                                                     IN: 2 
         OUT: out3                                                 ** out3 ** 
         ** out1 **                                                OUT: out1 

 LAYOUT nets out1(pin OUT) and out3(pin OUT) are cross-connected to instances X10[inv] and X30[inv]
